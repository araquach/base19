I officially began my hairdressing career in 2025 after starting out as a Saturday girl here at Base, which makes my journey feel even more special. I’ve always been creative growing up — whether that was fashion, art, musicals or performing — and hair has become my favourite way to channel all of that creativity into something people can wear and feel confident in every day.

<!-- block:section_1 -->
I’m especially passionate about bright, vivid colours and big transformations. I love how colour can completely shift how someone feels and how it allows people to show their personality. Creative colour really excites me and I’m inspired by artists who push boundaries and aren’t afraid to go bold.

Hairdressing also runs in my family, as my mum is a hairdresser too, so I’ve grown up understanding the dedication and heart that goes into this industry.

<!-- block:section_2 -->
For me, it’s not just about doing hair — it’s about the whole experience. I always want my clients to feel comfortable, listened to and genuinely excited about their appointment. When someone leaves feeling like the best version of themselves, that’s the best part of the job.

Outside of the salon, I’m usually spending time with friends and family, rewatching Harry Potter or Disney, walking my dog Bella or snowboarding, including racing and slopestyle, which keeps my adrenaline going!

<!-- block:section_3 -->
Long term, my goal is to grow into a full-time stylist within our sister salons and keep developing my skills, especially in creative colour. For now, I’m soaking up every opportunity to learn, build confidence, grow my social media and create hair that feels as bold and expressive as I am.
