I started my hairdressing journey as a Saturday girl while I was still in school and I’ve wanted to do this ever since. Being in the salon environment confirmed that this was exactly what I wanted for my career.

<!-- block:section_1 -->
I’m really looking forward to learning more advanced foiling techniques and different placement patterns. I love how small details can make such a big difference in the finished result and I’m excited to keep developing my skills.

One of my favourite things about being part of the Base team is how kind and helpful everyone is. Having experienced stylists around me who are always willing to guide and support makes such a difference while I’m learning.

<!-- block:section_2 -->
If I could choose any look for myself, it would be long, bright blonde hair — exactly what I have. I love that fresh, bold finish. My friends would describe me as friendly, creative and funny and I try to bring that same energy into the salon every day. You’ll usually find me powered by a vanilla matcha and a Kinder Bueno to keep me going through the busy days.

<!-- block:section_3 -->
Outside of work, I love shopping and going to concerts and festivals. Long term, my goal is to continue building my experience and one day open my own salon.
