From a young age, I knew hairdressing was where I was meant to be. I was the kid who wouldn’t let anyone else near my hair, not even my mum! I’ve always had a deep love for all things hair and self expression. Now, I’m turning that passion into a career as an Apprentice Stylist here at Base.

<!-- block:section_1 -->
The best part of my job? Definitely learning. I thrive in practical environments where I can be hands on and I'm especially loving learning foiling techniques. I find the complexity of colour work so exciting and I love seeing the transformations come to life through the process.

I started my journey early, working Saturdays at our sister salons PK & Jakata at just 14. That experience confirmed that the salon energy, the creativity and the constant learning were exactly what I needed. I’m not one to sit still at all, I need to be moving, doing, talking and hairdressing fits me perfectly.

<!-- block:section_2 -->
Outside of the salon, I’m either on the netball court, catching up with friends, or enjoying time with my family. I’m a big believer in living life with joy, staying true to yourself and spreading positive energy wherever you go.

My clients can expect more than just a service, they can expect a vibe. I’m talkative, upbeat and genuinely love making people feel great about themselves. Whether it’s through a killer blow dry or just a great chat, I aim to help people walk out feeling confident and cared for.

<!-- block:section_3 -->
Looking ahead, I dream of one day running my own salon, creating a space that’s as vibrant and welcoming as I aim to be every day. But for now, I’m soaking up every moment of this incredible opportunity at Base and giving it my all!
