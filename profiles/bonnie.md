I started my hairdressing journey because I’ve always loved hair and beauty and the way something as simple as a new style can completely change how someone feels. I enjoyed being creative at school and hairdressing felt like the perfect way to turn that creativity into something practical and meaningful. An apprenticeship was the right path for me because I learn best by doing.

<!-- block:section_1 -->
One of my favourite parts of joining the Base team has been learning from the senior stylists and seeing how much creativity and skill goes into every client’s hair. Everyone is so supportive and I love being part of a team where I can practise new techniques and grow every day. Being surrounded by people who genuinely want you to improve makes such a difference.

I’m especially looking forward to mastering highlights. I love how they can add brightness and dimension and completely transform someone’s hair. Learning different techniques like foiling and placement and understanding how to choose the right tones for each client really excites me.

<!-- block:section_2 -->
I’m inspired daily by the senior stylists across Base, Jakata and Paul Kemp. Watching their technique and how they connect with clients motivates me. I also get a lot of inspiration from American hairdressers online because their colour work and creativity are amazing. Seeing both in-salon role models and international stylists pushes me to keep learning and developing my own style.

For me, hairdressing is about more than just the finished result. I want my clients to feel comfortable, cared for and confident when they leave my chair. I’d describe myself as funny, creative and caring and I hope that comes through in the experience I give.

<!-- block:section_3 -->
Outside of the salon, I love going to the gym, staying active and spending time with friends. I also enjoy quieter moments like shopping, trying new foods or going to the cinema.

Long term, my goal is to build my skills, grow my own client base and one day run my own business, creating a space that feels welcoming and truly reflects my style.
