You’ll probably know me as Mais. I'm a warm-hearted Cancer with a big smile and an even bigger love for hairdressing. Becoming a stylist has been a dream of mine for as long as I can remember. I got my first taste of the industry doing a hairdressing course where I learned everything from head massages to tool handling. That was it for me — I was hooked.

<!-- block:section_1 -->
When I found out I had an interview at Base, I knew it was the next step in my journey and I was ready to give it everything. Now, as an apprentice, I’m loving every second of learning new skills, meeting amazing clients and helping them feel their absolute best.

My clients often tell me my head massages are next level — it’s a compliment I never get tired of hearing! But beyond that, I’m passionate about helping people feel confident, cared for and excited about their hair. That feeling when someone sees their dream hair has become a reality? I live for it.

<!-- block:section_2 -->
Off the salon floor, I’m all about travelling, discovering hidden gems and spending quality time with my friends and family. I find inspiration everywhere — in new places, meaningful conversations and the little moments that make life feel special.

What sets me apart? I’m super talkative, trustworthy and always up for a good laugh. I want every client I meet to feel totally at ease, like they’re hanging out with a friend who just happens to love creating beautiful hair.

<!-- block:section_3 -->
One day, I hope to continue growing into a confident stylist with a loyal clientele, someone known not just for her skills, but for the energy and kindness I bring into every appointment.
