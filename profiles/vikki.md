I'm a spirited hairstylist and educator with a deep love for all things hair and a heart full of passion. My life revolves around two incredible forces: **family and the vibrant world of hairstyling**. And with a boy and a girl who keep me endlessly entertained and on my toes, my world is a symphony of laughter, growth and love.

<!-- block:section_1 -->
When I'm not busy crafting **stunning hairstyles**, I'm immersing myself in the enchanting world of movies and TV shows, finding solace in the pages of a captivating book, or soaking up the energy of live music performances.

These moments of quiet reflection and shared experiences bring balance and inspiration to my life, fuelling my creativity and zest for the art of hairstyling.

My journey in the hairstyling realm spans over two decades — an exhilarating **22 years that have been marked by growth, learning and transformation**. It's a journey that I'm excited to continue as I take on the rewarding role of Lead Trainer and advanced stylist at Base Hairdressing.

<!-- block:section_2 -->
Guiding and shaping the talents of the next generation of stylists is a responsibility I carry with pride and dedication. My personal style can be described as a harmonious blend of **modern-vintage charm**, with a touch of methodical precision.

Beyond the salon, I channel my creativity into crafting intricate crochet garments, a testament to my eye for detail and knack for bringing ideas to life. And let's not forget my deep-rooted fascination with **vintage fashion** — a love affair that has spanned two decades.

<!-- block:section_3 -->
As a child, I found my passion for hairstyling in the most unexpected of places: **my beloved Barbie dolls**. Little did I know that those playful experiments would pave the way for a fulfilling career.

My journey took me through various salons, including a memorable stint at Haircuttery, where I honed my skills and evolved into a supervisor.

My path eventually led me to our sister salon Jakata, where I spent a fulfilling 11 years before being entrusted with the position of Lead Trainer at Base.
