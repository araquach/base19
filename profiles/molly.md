Hair, horses and a hint of wanderlust — that pretty much sums me up. I’ve loved hairdressing for as long as I can remember, from plaiting my friends' hair as a kid to now building my career as an Apprentice Stylist at Base.

<!-- block:section_1 -->
For me, hairdressing is all about connection. I love making people feel confident and cared for, and nothing beats the smile on a client’s face when they see their transformation.

I'm especially passionate about getting involved with blonding services — there’s something so satisfying about the detail and technique involved, and I can't wait to keep building on that.

<!-- block:section_2 -->
When I’m not in the salon, I’m either off exploring the world — I’ve been lucky to tick off a few dream spots already such as Thailand, Bali and Mauritius — or spending time with my three horses, Pip, Mishka and Nansi. Horses have been a huge part of my life, and producing my own has been one of my proudest accomplishments.

Clients say my head massages are next level and I’m always up for a good chat. I believe the best salon experiences aren’t just about the hair — they’re about how you feel while you’re in the chair.

<!-- block:section_3 -->
I want every appointment to feel relaxed, friendly and like you’re catching up with someone who really gets you.

As I grow in this industry, I’m dreaming big — not just about perfecting my craft, but about making a lasting impact in the lives of the people who sit in my chair.
